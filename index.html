Card Sleeves for Humanity makes the black cards with pick 2's and pick 3's much easier to play with!

No more skipping these troublesome black cards!

Card Sleeves for Humanity fixes these issues! The black cards that require 2 or more white cards can now easily be submitted in the correct order and shuffled so the anonymity of the submission is upheld.
